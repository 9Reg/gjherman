Book three

The conclusion

Erasure is also a form of power.

Delete is planned as the closing movement of the Loadtech sequence, bringing the trilogy back to its central concern: what remains of identity when memory, proof, and personhood can all be controlled.

The final book is designed to gather the sequence around the cost of survival in a system that can preserve what is useful, discard what is inconvenient, and call the result truth.

End state

  • ControlThe archive decides what counts.
  • AbsenceMissing memory becomes its own evidence.
