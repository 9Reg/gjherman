The conclusion

What happens when all memories are deleted?

Delete is the planned closing movement of the Loadtech trilogy. Events set in Download propogate through until they push the world to the brink of collapse.

What happens when the technology we rely on can turn us off like a lightswitch? What will people do to get that power? And what will they ask for once they have it?

Delete doesn't explore the natural progression of a civilization's decline, it explores the decay of a hero into the person he hates most. What would you do to save a loved one? Would you embrace the faults of a tyrant?

End state

  • PremiseThe villans have won.
  • GoalStop the destruction of the western world.
  • HowBy embracing tyranny.

Genre

Speculative thriller

A planned conclusion about how humanity embraces it's own decline. Post-cyberpunk becomes cyberpunk as the corporations we trust most start policing us.

Status

Planned manuscript

The third Loadtech novel closes the trilogy architecture begun in Download and expanded in Upload. Val, broken yet still ideological, needs to save the world, for he's the only one who can.
