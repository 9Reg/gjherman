Note: This book is on hold for a while. I've written about 300 pages for what will end up being The Fall (book 3) and book 4 (unnamed), and there are some damn good storylines in there, but no story yet. There's following Alba through the world after The Fall of civilization and the beginning of a new epoch (the catastrophocene--post catastrophe), to the lost of civil liberties following the rebuilding of society as Siberia and Africa split ways, to the history of how 12 and her sisters were created, all great storylines. But nothing yet to bind them together. And, I'm having too much fun with Shudder II (The Harvest) right now.
